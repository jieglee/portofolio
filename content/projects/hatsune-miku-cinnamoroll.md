# 💙 Hatsune Miku × Cinnamoroll

A digital illustration of **Hatsune Miku** reimagined in the adorable chibi style of **Cinnamoroll** — two beloved Japanese pop culture icons combined into one cute character design.

## 💡 Concept

This illustration was born from a love of both Vocaloid culture and Sanrio's aesthetic. The idea was to blend Hatsune Miku's iconic teal twin-tails, headset, and futuristic outfit with Cinnamoroll's soft, round, and kawaii chibi proportions.

The result is a chibi Miku sitting casually — complete with her signature color palette of teal and grey, holding a drink, with a tiny butterfly nearby and her Cinnamoroll-inspired bow on top.

## 🎨 Design Details

- **Style** — Chibi / Kawaii illustration
- **Color Palette** — Teal (`#39C5BB`), grey, white, with soft pastel shading
- **Character** — Hatsune Miku in Cinnamoroll chibi proportions
- **Details Included:**
  - Miku's signature teal twin-tails with red/grey headset
  - Cinnamoroll-style bow on hair
  - Classic Miku outfit — grey top, teal tie, dark skirt, black thigh-highs
  - Casual sitting pose with a drink cup
  - Small butterfly detail near her foot
